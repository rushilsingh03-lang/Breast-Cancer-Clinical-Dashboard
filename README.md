# Breast Cancer Clinical Dashboard

## Overview

This interactive dashboard was developed as part of the **Breast Cancer Clinical Outcome Analysis** project using the **METABRIC Breast Cancer Dataset**. It provides a visual summary of the key findings obtained from SQL-based analysis and survival analysis, enabling users to explore patient demographics, tumour characteristics, treatment patterns, and clinical outcomes through interactive visualizations.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard was designed to:

- Present a high-level overview of the patient cohort.
- Visualize tumour characteristics and treatment distributions.
- Explore survival outcomes across different clinical variables.
- Enable interactive filtering for dynamic data exploration.
- Support data-driven interpretation of breast cancer clinical data.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Clinical Overview

This page summarizes the overall patient cohort and includes:

- Patient demographics
- Tumour stage distribution
- Cancer type distribution
- Breast surgery distribution
- Chemotherapy and hormone therapy comparison
- Interactive slicers for filtering the data

---

### Page 2 – Survival & Clinical Insights

This page focuses on patient outcomes and includes:

- Average overall survival by patient status
- Tumour stage, mutation count, and survival relationship
- Nottingham Prognostic Index (NPI) risk distribution
- Overall survival status
- ER and HER2 status comparison
- Interactive clinical filters
